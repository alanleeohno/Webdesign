Oil Painting

Five of the world's most expensive oil paintings, ranked No. 1 of course, ranked No. 2 unexpectedly

Name Picture Detail
《救世主》(The Savior) The first one is Leonardo Da Vinci. Everyone takes it for granted, because Leonardo is a rare all-round genius. He is not only good at painting, but also good at scientific and technological inventions. And he's very good at crossing the border!
《交换》(Exchange) The painting was $300 million, but it was hard to appreciate. In fact, his work style has always been like this, full of expressive force, but there is no specific image, is a typical Abstract expressionism.
《玩纸牌的人》(The Player of Cards) The painting was $300 million, but it was hard to appreciate. In fact, his work style has always been like this, full of expressive force, but there is no specific image, is a typical Abstract expressionism.
《你何时结婚？》(When do you get married? ") This painting was created by the artist when he went to live in seclusion on Tahiti Island. In the painting, two local girls wear bright clothes and flowers on their heads. The color of the picture is very strong, giving people a sense of hope. And this sentence is more profound, representing the painter's yearning for life.
《No.17A》 The painting fetched a high price of $200 million. Many people said that they could not understand his works. In fact, his paintings dripped out, not painted. From the artistic form, he is more inclined to expressionism, and from the content, his paintings are more abstract, so he is called abstract expressionism.
