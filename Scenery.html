维多利亚瀑布(Victoria Falls) Located in the border area between Zambia and Zimbabwe in southern Africa, at the junction of the upper and middle reaches of the Zambezi River, it is the largest waterfall in Africa and one of the largest, most beautiful and spectacular waterfalls in the world. This waterfall, can be said to be the "light of Africa", the Zimbabwean government is even considering the construction of Victoria Falls Special Economic Zone. In addition, in the last issue, the most dangerous swimming pool in the world we introduced was at the top of it.
伊瓜苏瀑布(Cataratas del Iguazú) Iguazu Falls is the world's widest waterfall, so it is also known as the "mother of the world's waterfalls". As a world natural heritage, it belongs to one of the "New Seven Wonders of Nature" in the world. Located at 23 km upstream of the confluence of the Iguazu and Parana rivers on the border between Argentina and Brazil, it is 82 meters high and 4 kilometers wide, with an average drop of 75 meters. Perhaps even more well-known is that in the collaboration between brother Zhang Guorong and Liang Chaowei's Spring Break, their agreed destination is this waterfall.
尼亚加拉瀑布(Niagara Falls) As the world's first large-span waterfall, it is located at the junction of Ontario, Canada, and New York, USA. The waterway is 56 kilometers long, from 174 meters above sea level to 75 meters above sea level. The main waterfall is located in Canada, which is the best place to watch the waterfall; the waterfall in the United States is on the side of the waterfall. With the above mentioned Victoria Falls, Iguazu Falls, and known as the world's three large-span national waterfalls. Its scenery is also shocked and beautiful beyond words.
安赫尔瀑布(Angel Falls) Known by the public, it may be due to the animated film "Flying House Travel" in previous years. The director put the wonderful scenery of this place into the film, which shocked many people for a while. Flying House Tour is a picture of a waterfall on the Cullen River, a tributary of the Caroni River on the Guyana Plateau in Bolivar, Venezuela. The fall of Angel Falls is 979 meters, and its bottom width is 150 meters. It is the highest waterfall in the world. Because the waterfall is covered by many vegetation, it is most wonderful to see from the air. There are also related projects that will give you a glimpse of the beauty of the world's first falls in the air. Especially in the morning and evening, clouds and sunshine fall like a golden galaxy. Of course, from June to December of each year, you can also travel along the river, because this is a flood season, no matter from which angle to appreciate the waterfall, to experience, the waterfall is very beautiful.
黄金瀑布(Gullfoss) This year's World Cup, Iceland is in the limelight. Of course, they are not only the "Viking War roar", but also the most famous one is Iceland's cool style and beautiful scenery. Here is the beauty of seasons. Spring, summer, autumn and winter, there are different beauty, the stars aurora, not to mention. It can be said that the golden waterfall is the most beautiful waterfall in the world. Among them, the golden waterfall, as the best beauty spot in Iceland, is regarded by the locals as the queen of many waterfalls in Iceland. Located 125 kilometers northeast of the capital Reykjavik, it is 2500 meters wide and 70 meters high. It is the largest fault gorge waterfall in Iceland. Behind its beautiful background, hydropower development has been considered. Fortunately, these plans have not been realized, or they will become the next "Setkaidas Falls" and be completely destroyed. Today, the Golden Falls have become a National Nature Reserve and one of Iceland's most popular tourist attractions.
德天瀑布(Detian waterfall) As the first waterfall in Asia, it is located in Detian Village, Shuolong Town, Daxin County, Chongzuo City, Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region. Because it is located at the border between China and Vietnam, and connected with the adjacent Vietnamese Banyo Falls, it is the fourth largest national waterfall in the world. The average annual water flow is more than three times that of Huangguoshu Waterfall in Guizhou, but it is estimated that the current Huangguoshu Waterfall is more than three times that of the past. Because of so many resounding titles, it has become the scene of many movies and TV dramas. The Fire's Flowers and Bones was shot here before.
